# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in Optedge welcoming and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, education, appearance, disability, nationality, race, religion, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, or socioeconomic status.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Discuss evidence and implementation choices without attacking people.
- Accept correction and acknowledge uncertainty.
- Protect other people's privacy and financial information.
- Keep disagreements focused on the project's research, engineering, and safety goals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or sexualized attention.
- Trolling, insults, personal attacks, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing another person's private information, including financial or broker data.
- Pressuring contributors to make profit promises, conceal adverse evidence, or bypass safety controls.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional community.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation for behavior they judge inappropriate, threatening, or harmful. Enforcement decisions should be proportional, documented privately where practical, and focused on protecting the community.

Report conduct concerns through a private channel listed on the [maintainer's GitHub profile](https://github.com/kaiuribess). Do not include credentials, account data, or unrelated private information. Reports will be reviewed and handled as privately as reasonably possible.
